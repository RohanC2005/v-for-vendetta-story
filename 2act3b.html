ROOFTOP OBSERVATION

From above, you see the city as Norsefire intended: orderly, silent, obedient.

Yet pockets of defiance flicker like candle flames in the dark.

Evey is gone, a ghost in the system, and you feel the weight of isolation.

The city hums below as plans for disruption take form.

Your moves tonight must be precise; the balance is fragile.
