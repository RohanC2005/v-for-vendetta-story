THE INVESTIGATION

Inspector Finch studies every word you tell him.

He doesn’t interrupt much. He simply listens, eyes narrowing as the story unfolds.

Other officers talk about terrorists.

Finch asks about Larkhill.

For a moment the room becomes very quiet.

A — Finch begins investigating Larkhill records. The government archives suddenly become very important. B — Doubt grows while you describe the masked man. Something about the official story feels incomplete. C — Curiosity wins and you begin searching for V again. The investigation continues behind you.
