THE MAN IN THE MASK

Rain drips down the narrow alley walls.

Evey’s arms still ache where the Fingermen held her. Their laughter hasn’t left her ears yet, echoing somewhere deep inside her head.

Then the masked man appeared.

Steel flashed.

Now the men lie motionless on the pavement while the stranger stands calmly beside her.

Sirens begin waking the city.

Evey can’t decide whether she has just been saved… or pulled into something worse.
