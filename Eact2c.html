THE SILENT AFTERMATH

Blue police lights fill the alley.

Questions arrive quickly about the explosion and the dead Fingermen. Evey answers slowly, her voice sounding strange even to herself.

The officers exchange uneasy looks.

Someone quietly writes down a name.

V.

For the first time Evey realizes the man who saved her might already be part of something much larger.

A — Investigators study your testimony carefully. The search for the masked attacker spreads across London. B — Hours later you step outside alone. The memory of the mask still refuses to fade. C — The ruined courthouse draws you back through dark streets. Smoke and rubble remain.
