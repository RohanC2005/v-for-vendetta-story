THE TEST

Evey awakens inside a cold prison cell.

Days pass under interrogation and isolation.

Every question demands the same answer.

Betray the masked man.

But she refuses.

Only then does the truth reveal itself.

The prison was never real.

It was your creation.

Evey steps into the rain again, finally free from fear.

A — Guide Evey through the tunnels beneath Parliament. B — Move through the tunnels alone and trigger the detonator yourself. C — Meet Inspector Finch in the streets before the final act.
