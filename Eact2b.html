RUNNING THROUGH THE CITY

Evey runs until her chest burns.

Behind her the explosion rolls across the skyline like thunder. Sirens spread across London, growing louder every second.

Her legs slow.

Her thoughts don’t.

The image of the mask keeps returning.

She doesn’t know whether to feel grateful… or terrified.
