THE SHADOW GALLERY

The underground room feels impossible.

Paintings cover the walls. Music hums softly somewhere unseen. Shelves overflow with books the government banned years ago.

Evey moves carefully between them.

Her shoulders stay tight.

Every small sound makes her flinch.

The masked man finally speaks.

“Call me V.”

A — V begins revealing the history Norsefire erased. Forbidden records quietly rewrite everything you believed. B — Your voice rises suddenly, accusing the masked man of hiding violence behind elegant speeches. C — The gallery remains silent while you study everything around you. The masked man simply watches.
