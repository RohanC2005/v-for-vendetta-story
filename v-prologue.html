PROLOGUE — A CONTROLLED CITY

London no longer argues with its rulers.

The Norsefire regime governs every broadcast, every newspaper, every whisper that travels through the streets.

Surveillance towers blink like watchful eyes above the rooftops. Curfew sirens mark the passing hours like a heartbeat of authority.

Most citizens have learned the safest habit of all. Silence.

But beneath that silence something is moving.

Files buried in forgotten archives. Names erased from government records. A detention facility once known as Larkhill.

History will remember this night as the beginning.

But right now…

It begins with a woman walking home through the rain.
